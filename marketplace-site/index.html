#Live from the relays snapshot

What actually announces itself on Nostr today, read from public relays (relay.damus.io, nos.lol, relay.primal.net): Routstr inference providers (kind 38421) and ecash mints (NIP-87, kinds 38172/38173) with their signed reviews (kind 38000). These are announcements as published, not endorsements — and announcements outlive their nodes, so each clearnet endpoint is probed when the snapshot refreshes (dead ≠ delisted; onion-only endpoints can't be probed from here and show as unverified). Today's signed reviews target the mints, not the providers — verify a provider or mint before trusting it.

The same model is priced differently across providers. The widest-served models, cheapest provider first — full index, every model and every alive provider, at /live/models.json. Prices are the providers' own published numbers in sats per 1M tokens; max_cost (in the index) is each provider's per-request ceiling — the budgeting number.
